    Summer Program
    (Limited enrollment for students 8 -15 years of age)

    In order to provide a comprehensive educational experience, we limit our enrollment to twelve students per weekly session. Our summer program begins in June and continues into August. We offer weekly sessions, which are scheduled from 9 – 4 Monday through Friday.

    Students will receive a mounted group lesson daily and participate in all aspects of stable management and equine care. In addition, there will be daily classroom instruction which is based on the US Pony Club Manual, and swimming for those who wish to cool off!

    Cost per session is $400.00. Two consecutive sessions $700.00.

    Pony Party

    We will come to you or you can come to us on our 10 acre facility. We would be honored to celebrate with you! One pony is $225 per hour and a second pony can be added for $100. If you wish, videos or photographs of professional quality can be made available to preserve your memories.
