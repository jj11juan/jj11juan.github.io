Our Summer Program



Limited Enrollment

Ages 8-15

In order to provide a comprehensive educational experience, we limit our enrollment to twelve students per weekly session. Our summer program begins in June and continues through August. We offer weekly sessions, which are scheduled from 9 - 4 Monday through Friday.

Students will receive a mounted group lesson daily and participate in all aspects of stable management and equine care. In addition, there will be daily classroom instruction which is based on the US Pony Club Manual.

Cost per session is $400.00.
Two consecutive sessions $750.00.
